Research

My research sits at the intersection of software engineering, software security, and applied LLM. I focus on (1) understanding and improving the security of LLM-generated code, (2) building evaluation frameworks and benchmarks for code generation systems, and (3) applying large language models to core SE tasks such as test generation, vulnerability detection, and automated repair.
